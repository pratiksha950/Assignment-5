Heres a classic Malpua recipe :a sweet, soft, and crispy Indian pancake.

Instructions:

1. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, mix flour, semolina, sugar, fennel, cardamom, and dry fruits. Slowly add milk to make a smooth, thick pouring batter (like dosa batter). Let it rest for 30 minutes (optional but helps texture).

2. Make sugar syrup: In a pan, boil sugar and water until slightly sticky (1-string consistency). Add cardamom and saffron. Turn off the heat. Add rose water/kewra for aroma (optional).

3. Fry Malpua: Heat ghee in a shallow pan. Pour a ladle of batter into hot ghee :it will spread into a round shape. Cook on medium heat until golden and crispy on both sides. Remove and dip immediately into warm sugar syrup for 1-2 minutes. Take it out and let excess syrup drain.
