Here's a simple and delicious Paneer recipe for you:

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili. Sauté for a minute until raw smell disappears.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook till soft and mushy.
5. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates from the masala.
6. Add paneer cubes and mix gently to coat them in the masala.
7. Sprinkle garam masala and a little water if you prefer a semi-dry bhaji. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
